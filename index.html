ABOUT THE EVENT

WHAT IS PRAYATNA?

The Symposium

Prayatna is an inter-college technical symposium at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, PSG College of Technology. Spanning two days, it brings together undergraduate students from the mechanical stream for competitions, workshops, and exhibitions — a dynamic platform to showcase technical expertise and innovative thinking.

Our Vision

To become a flagship event inspiring the next generation of engineers to pursue excellence in manufacturing and mechanical engineering, bridging academic learning and industry practices while promoting sustainable and innovative solutions.

Our Mission

To foster innovation, technical proficiency, and collaborative learning among engineering students by providing a platform to solve real-world problems and connect with industry leaders.

THINKSPIRE '26

THINKSPIRE ’26 is an innovation idea presentation event that provides a platform for students to showcase their creative and technical skills by presenting innovative solutions to real-world problems. Got an innovative project or idea worth showcasing? This event provides a platform for students to present their recent projects, research, or innovative concepts. Participants will submit an abstract of their work, and shortlisted teams will present their projects before a panel of judges to compete for the best innovation award.

MECHANICAL AUTOPSY

Mechanical Autopsy is a two-round technical event that challenges participants to analyze and deconstruct a mechanical product to understand its components, materials, and manufacturing processes. Teams will reverse engineer the given object and propose an innovative improvement based on functionality, feasibility, and cost effectiveness.

LOCKED IN

LOCKED IN is a multi-stage technical escape room challenge where teams of 2–3 enter a completely dark room and solve three intense stages of puzzles. Participants must combine logic, teamwork, and technical thinking to unlock the final code and escape before time runs out.

GUESSTIMATOR

Guesstimator is a thrilling battle of logic, estimation, and risk-taking. In teams of two, participants will answer time-bound questions by wagering tokens based on their confidence in the answer. Strategy, intuition, and smart decision-making will determine who survives and who gets eliminated. The team with the highest tokens at the end wins.
